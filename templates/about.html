About Our Expert System for Diagnosis

Welcome to our project, "Expert System for the confirmation of Diagnosis using Lab Results." This initiative is designed to assist medical professionals in accurately assigning ICD-10 codes based on patient lab values, age, and gender.

Project Overview

Our system leverages a sophisticated rule-based approach to evaluate lab results and other patient data, providing a reliable tool for diagnosing and coding diseases. The system is designed to be scalable and adaptable, allowing for the easy integration of new rules and updates as medical knowledge evolves.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of medical coding by automating the assignment of ICD-10 codes. We aim to support healthcare providers in making informed decisions quickly and accurately, thereby improving patient care and outcomes.

Team 9

Assigned to this project, Team 9 is comprised of dedicated professionals with expertise in medical informatics, software development, and healthcare management. Together, we are committed to delivering a robust and user-friendly solution that meets the needs of medical experts worldwide.

Quick Hint:

When creating rules in our system:

Between Conditions: The system uses an AND operator, meaning all conditions must be met for the rule to be true.

Between Rules: The system uses an OR operator, meaning any one of the rules can trigger a diagnosis if its conditions are satisfied.

This ensures precise control over how diagnoses are confirmed using multiple lab values, age, and gender criteria.
